Overview

    This site will be covering topics about social media The point that I hope to get across is the importance and impact of republishable social media. The main focal point of my work is on Twitter, as retweeting is a powerful tool for branding of companies and pushing opinions and ideas of social justice. This is done through retweting and hashtag campaigns, allowing users of both large and small followings to have a loud voice in the overwhelming world we live in.
    I will go over hashtags campaings including #BlackLivesMatter & #MeToo, along with other ideas. My thesis is that younger generations and their actions geared in these topics are more greatly influenced by republishable social media such as Twitter rather than social media such as Snapchat or Instagram, and therefore are more likely to spread information in general more effectively than any generation before.
